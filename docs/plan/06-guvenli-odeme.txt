================================================================================
ESCROW ODEME AKISI
================================================================================

1. Yuk veren tasiyici secer
2. Odeme sisteme aktarilir
   -> ESCROW_FUNDS_LOCKED
3. Para escrow hesabinda tutulur
4. Tasima baslar
5. Pickup QR okutulur
6. GPS + device + QR dogrulanir
7. Shipment process baslar
8. Milestone QR’lari kullanilir (varsa)
9. Final delivery QR okutulur
10. AI fraud analizi calisir
11. Odeme release edilir
   -> ESCROW_FUNDS_RELEASED

================================================================================
MILESTONE PAYMENT SISTEMI
================================================================================

Büyük lojistik operasyonlar icin:

ORNEK:
Toplam 100.000 TL

- %30 Pickup
- %40 Transit checkpoint
- %30 Final delivery

HER MILESTONE:
- ayrilmis escrow lock
- ayrilmis QR token
- ayrilmis fraud score
- ayrilmis release karari

KURAL:
Her milestone bagimsiz finansal unit olarak davranir.

================================================================================
QR DOGRULAMA SISTEMI
================================================================================

QR TOKEN YAPISI:
{
  shipmentId,
  driverId,
  customerId,
  timestamp,
  nonce,
  signature,
  checkpointType
}

GUVENLIK:
- AES-256-GCM encryption
- HMAC-SHA256 signature
- 15 dakika TTL
- single-use QR
- replay protection

================================================================================
QR DOGRULAMA AKISI
================================================================================

1. QR generate edilir
2. Driver QR okutur
3. Sistem kontrol eder:
   - JWT auth
   - nonce validity
   - TTL kontrolu
   - GPS validation
   - device fingerprint
4. Basarili:
   -> QR_SCANNED event
5. Basarisiz:
   -> QR_SCAN_FAILED event
